# 💭 Worry Dump & Release

A playful little web app for letting go of what's on your mind — dramatically.

**👉 [Try it live](https://lilyj8D.github.io/worry-dump/)**

## What is this?

Write down whatever's bothering you, then choose how to release it:

- 🔥 **Burn it** — watch your words sizzle away
- 🎈 **Yeet it** — send them floating into the sky
- ⚽ **Shoot it** — kick your worry into a goal (you get 5 tries!)

Nothing is saved. Ever. Your words disappear for good.

## Why?

Sometimes you just need to get something off your chest. This app makes that feel satisfying — and maybe even a little funny.

Built to be family-friendly. Do it with your kids, share it with your team, or just use it solo after a long day.

## Features

- 🎮 Interactive penalty shootout mini-game
- 💬 Funny sound effects and commentary
- 📱 Mobile-friendly — works on any phone browser
- 🔒 Zero data storage — nothing is tracked or saved
- 🎨 Warm, playful design

## Tech

A single `index.html` file. No frameworks, no build tools, no backend. Just open it and go.
